4 cards are displayed at a time. Try to make 24 by combining the cards with +, -, *, /, (, and ).
You will be assigned a buzzer. Press it when you are ready to answer.
You will receive 1 point for a correct solution. You will lose 1 point for a wrong solution.
If no solution is possible, there will be no change to the score.
The game ends when all cards in the deck are played or if there are no possible solutions left.
Press the buzzer and enter the correct solution before your opponent to win!
